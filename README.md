# Afficionado Coffee Roasters – Temporal Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing transaction-level retail data from Afficionado Coffee Roasters to identify temporal sales patterns across different hours, days, and store locations. The objective of the project is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights that support operational optimization and data-driven decision-making.

The analysis was performed using Microsoft Excel and Power BI, with interactive dashboards developed to visualize customer demand behavior, sales trends, peak transaction hours, and store-level performance.

---

## Business Problem

Afficionado Coffee Roasters lacked a centralized analytical system to monitor temporal sales behavior effectively. Operational decisions such as staffing allocation and scheduling were primarily based on assumptions rather than quantitative evidence.

This project addresses the following challenges:

* Overstaffing during low-demand periods
* Understaffing during rush hours
* Lack of visibility into peak transaction times
* Inefficient operational planning
* Inconsistent customer experience

---

## Project Objectives

### Primary Objectives

* Identify overall sales trends across 2025
* Determine busiest and slowest days of the week
* Identify peak transaction hours

### Secondary Objectives

* Compare demand behavior across store locations
* Support workforce scheduling optimization
* Provide operational recommendations based on data insights

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains transaction-level coffee retail data including:

* transaction_id
* transaction_time
* transaction_qty
* unit_price
* store_location
* product_category
* product_type
* product_detail

Derived analytical features:

* Revenue
* Hour of transaction
* Day of week
* Time buckets (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Late Hours)

## Key Insights

* Morning hours generated the highest transaction volume and revenue.
* Weekend sales significantly outperformed weekday sales.
* Customer behavior varied across store locations.
* Afternoon periods experienced relatively lower demand.
* Peak sales periods were concentrated during breakfast hours.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Increase staffing during morning rush hours.
* Reduce staffing during low-demand periods.
* Implement store-specific operational strategies.
* Launch promotional campaigns during slow hours.
* Use dashboard analytics for real-time monitoring.
